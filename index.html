Birth and Rebirth Cycle

  • It was born in 1933

    • Only the second building project of its kind Model City was even more diverse than it is today. It was home to all the "other colors" that the laws of the day segregated. Miami was the home of the working man post depression. Everyone was coming into Miami for the steady construction work. As the Miami economy and public services were leagues above the rural establishment of the time. During, this period American Blacks the "South" and long time residents from Overtown began to pour in.

  • Lyndon Johnson's effect on south florida in 1966

    • This was the time when Liberty City had the most Middle-Class flight. After, the covenant housing laws ending so to did the communities exposer to middle-class leadership Read about other "Model Cites"Widespread urban violence, disillusionment with existing urban renewal programmes, and bureaucratic difficulties in the first years of the War on Poverty led to calls for reform of federal programmes. The Model Cities initiative created a new program at the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) intended to improve coordination of existing urban programs.

  • Authorized to Riot, Liberty City resident declare War on Miami

    • Basically, a well known story about racism and legal abuse of power, is that of Author McDuffie. Police (if they even could be called such) were a necessary evil in Miami. The United States was under siege in the last sixties and early seventies by a Drug Trade that was spilling over its Florida border. So national money was set a side to accelerate the number of police in the county to match the outbreaks of violence facing Miami for the very first time since its founding. Great and illustrious criminial made millions from corrupting this new police force. Author McDuffie's murder was only a symptom of the cancer of Miami's legal arms. However, let me be perfectly clear the response was equally violent and abusing. People were murdered and the foundation of Government was marched upon with violent intent. Finally, war powers were evoked and the riots ended but with the City of Miami forever tramatized by its inability to converge openly with culturally different communities.

    • All things must end. Even curses are one day broken. In 2015, the City was again experiencing the pangs of social poverty caused primarily the citizens financial and intellectual fragility. Too many single mom, war veterans, pastors, and children live in reside in a neighborhood where random crime and poor decisions are common. Someone brought out the Landlord's stake in the property. The plan for this space is to accelerate the people's heart's and minds who live there. An environment that will gentrify historically impoverished from the inside out.
